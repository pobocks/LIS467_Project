Directions:

From north or south on Hwy 51 (I-39), take the Hwy 64 exit westward. Follow Hwy 64 through town about 2 miles. Hwy 64 joins with First St. just after the Prairie River Middle School. The Library will be on the right.

From east on Hwy 64: Follow Hwy 64 through town about 2 miles. Hwy 64 joins with First St. just after the Prairie River Middle School. The Library will be on the right.

From west on Hwy 64/107: Follow Hwy 64 (W. Main St.) It joins First St. at the Prairie River Bridge. At the bridge there is a three-way stop. Across the bridge on the left is the Library.
